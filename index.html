Misunderstanding Stoicism
The definition of the word stoic often directly misrepresents the philosophy of Stoicism, and it is understandable that one would make that mistake. The words do share a commonality in some regards. The dictionary definition of stoic is one who is indifferent to pleasure or pain. This leads many to believe that Stoicism encourages people to cut off all emotion and avoid all pleasure.
